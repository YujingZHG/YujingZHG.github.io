Website Topic

My-Homepage

  • Why I choose this topic? I'm excited to develop my own website as a canvas for my art and photography hobby and share my idea. When I make a new friend and talked about hobbies, you know it is annoying to find pictures from different platform and the photo folders on the phone, because they are usually mix with other photos. I really need a webpage working as my “business card”.
  • And it is not just about showing my work, it's about creating a personal connection. My website will be a place where I can share my latest drawings and photos, without the limitations of social media apps, and the inconvenience of accessibility platforms across different countries. This digital space will reflects my growth offering a glimpse into my life and thoughts. It's important to me that this website serves as a more accessible and direct way to my friends, family, and anyone interested in my art, without the compression or algorithmic barriers of social platforms.
  • The mission is building a site that not only displays my work in the way I want but also becomes a part of my journey, constantly updated with new pieces and experiences. This project is a meaningful way to express myself and connect with others over shared interests.
