Balloon Festival in Canberra

Photography by Yujing

The Canberra Balloon Festival is a big event in Australia where many hot air balloons fly in the sky. It happens every year in March in Canberra. People can see many different balloons early in the morning. It's a fun time with food and music for families to enjoy.
